“There is no greater joy, nor greater reward than to make a fundamental difference in someone's life.”

We believe that no amount is ever too little to make a difference.



We are a group of highly passionate individuals, aiming to make an application that has a lasting impact in the world. Keeping the less privileged in mind, we want to empower local charities by placing them on this common platform. This bridges the gap between Singaporeans and local charities. Singaporeans may not be actively donating to local charities because they do not know what charities exist, but now they can find out easily. There is no excuse to not take action to help the less privileged in the community now.


Donating even a cent to the verified charities listed on our page, can change their lives.
